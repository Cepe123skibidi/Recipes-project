How to make pizza

Do you ever wake up like Im feeling Italian, well then lets make a pizza.

  1. Get a cup of warm water
  2. Add a 1 packet of yeast & 1 teaspoon of sugar
  3. Let sit for 5-10 minutes
  4. In the meantime get 2 1/4 cup of bread flower or all purpose flower and put it into a big bowl
  5. Add 1 teaspoon of salt into the big bowl
  6. Once the yeast is done add it to the big bowl
  7. Add 2 table spoons of olive oil
  8. Mix untill its stretchy
  9. Let dough rest
  10. Put flower on the counter
  11. Put dough on the flower
  12. Shape and place on parchment paper
  13. (Add sauce
  14. Add cheese
  15. Add peperoni)
    • Basicaly just add toppings
  16. Put on sheet tray and oven for 15 mins

One last thing to remember before you add more toppings throw away the pinapple you are not putting it on your pizza!
