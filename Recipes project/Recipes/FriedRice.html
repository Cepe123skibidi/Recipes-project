How to make Fried Rice

I dont even know what to say fried rice just sounds yummy.

  1. First add 2/3 cups of baby carots to a bowl
  2. Boil on low for 5 mins
  3. Add 1/2 frozen green peas
  4. Then drain the water
  5. Get a pan and add 2 tea spoons of vegetable oil
  6. Throw vegetables on the pan and heat
  7. Add to eggs and stir
  8. Add 3 cups of white rice
  9. Add 1 table spoon of soysauce (optional)
  10. Add 2 table spoons of sesame oil
  11. Stir and serve

Now that we have made fried rice we can enjoy it, and feel free to add chicken for protein.
