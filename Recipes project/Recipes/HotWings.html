How to Make Hot Wings

Are you craving some spicy goodness, if yes lets make some hot wings.

  1. Get chicken wings
  2. Cut off wing tip and seperate the drummet and flat with a knife (you can also buy them pre-seperated)
  3. Get a bowl of flower
  4. Add some paprika, cayenne, and salt to your flower (most of the flavor will come form the buffalo sauce so dont overdo it)
  5. Sprinkle the flower over the wings
  6. Cover and leave in the fridge for about an hour
  7. While its in the fridge lets heat some vegetable oil to 375 on the stove
  8. For the sauce get a new bowl on the stove and add 2 sticks of butter
  9. Pour hot sauce on the butter (I recomend alot of hot sauce)
  10. Melt over low
  11. Add some garlic powder and black pepper (feel free to add whatever seasoning you want)
  12. Stir constantly and keep warm while we fry the wings
  13. Place small batches of wings in the oil (it should take about 10-15 minutes or till they're golden)
  14. Drain on paper towels before you toss them in the sauce
  15. Put the sauce you made on the wings
  16. Dont forget a nice cold drink to go with your wings

Now that we have made our wings its time to dig in. LETS GET MESSY!
